Why I love Winter: A short story

#winter #love #snow #january

Roshni sb k lye started in 2020. We have a vision to spread education to those who cannot get it due to their financial crisis, and to meet their financial situation they have to indulge in the curse of child labor. Our mission is to provide them quality education and financial support. Roshni sb k laiy works to promote and protect the rights of children in Pakistan, we are bringing basic services including free quality education and financial support to those who are most in need.

With nearly 5 million of the country population under 10 years, Pakistan has over 80 millions child. This makes it imparitive for programmes interventions and policies to champion the rights of child affectively Roshni sb k laiy is committed to supporting the society to ensure that all girls and boys brought up well educated.

Roshni sb k lye is supported by contributions from society around the lahore and private NGO. Our major funding partner is waduha welfare organization. A concept brought to the education by synergycluster is Roshni Sb K Liay
